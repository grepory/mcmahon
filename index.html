Own Your Availability

Clouds are replacing datacenters, containers are replacing VMs and microservices are replacing monolithic applications. Independent microservices help engineering organizations break free of the quarterly release cycle and ship small increments of functionality every single day. There's a cost, though. What once ran in a single process now spans multiple machines, and unreliable infrastructure can add new failure modes between you and your users.

Now, more than ever, application developers are being asked to own the availability of their code. We're here to help.

Made for Microservices

Opsee is made for distributed environments. Hundreds of services in production? No problem. Opsee recognizes new instances and containers as they come online and automatically starts health checking their services.

No Software, no Agent

No bash scripts to blithely curl and pipe into sudo sh, and no opaque APT repos to add. Our bastion instance is delivered as a prebuilt AMI that automatically detects your AWS infrastructure and services, and coordinates health checking and incident response.

Built to Work Anywhere

Being on call can be a real drag. All too often your sleep will get ruined by an easily solved problem like a noisy threshold or a malfunctioning VM. Opsee can help you do everything from silencing a noisy alert to restarting a crashed process, all from your phone.

Designed for You

Better anomaly detection, prettier graphs and more data are all nice, but how many new devops products feel like they were designed for the people who use them? It shouldn’t take a webinar to explain how our tools work.

Frontend Developer

Opsee is looking for a talented, senior frontend engineer to be our second employee and lead the development of our web application. You’ll be using your expertise in Javascript and modern JS frameworks (like Angular or React) to build tools developers love.

You’ll work closely with the backend team to spec and build new features, and process real-time data streams (WebSockets) from our APIs and bastion hosts. You’ll also work with design to create a seamless and delightful product experience, and a powerful web application to notify users of problems and visualize and report on their application health and performance.

To apply, send an email with your résumé and a little about yourself to work@opsee.co.
